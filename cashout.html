How to Cash Out your Earnings!
  1. Check Your Balance
    • At the top of the page, you’ll see your current available balance. Make sure you have enough funds to cash out. The minimum withdrawal amount is ₦1000.
  2. Enter the Amount to Cash Out
    • In the "Amount" field, type the exact amount you want to withdraw. Ensure it's within your available balance and meets the minimum withdrawal requirement.
  3. Select a Payment Method
    • Choose how you would like to receive your funds:
      • Bank Transfer: Provide your bank account details.
      • Mobile Wallet: Enter your mobile number or email associated with your wallet (e.g., Paystack or Flutterwave).
  4. Fill in Your Payment Details
    • Depending on the payment method you selected, enter the required details such as:
      • For Bank Transfer: Account Number and Bank Name.
      • For Mobile Wallet: Mobile Number or Email for your wallet.
  5. Submit Your Cashout Request
    • After filling in the form, click the "Cash Out Now" button to submit your request.
    • You’ll see a confirmation page summarizing your cashout details. Review the information carefully and click "Confirm Cashout" to proceed.
  6. Wait for Processing
    • Once submitted, your request will be processed. Cashout requests are typically processed within 24 hours. You can check the status of your request in the Cashout History section.
  7. Check Your Cashout History
    • Track your previous cashout requests under the "Cashout History" section to view the status of each request, including completed and pending transactions.
